L7 / L9

概述
LS708AU可通过自带的一组液晶显示器、键盘、触摸鼠标访问多台计算机，一台LS708AU可以直接管理8台计算机，通过级联匹配的切换器，最多可控制 248 台计算机；额外提供一组本地控制接口，可同步检视与管控服务器。
LS708AU集成了LED背光的LCD 液晶显示器、键盘、触摸鼠标于 1U高度的控制终端，节省机房使用空间并提高了工作效率。

The LS708AU / LS716AU allows access to multiple computers through its built-in LCD monitor, keyboard, and touchpad. One LS708AU/ LS716AU can directly manage 8/16 computers, and through cascaded switchers, it can control up to 256 computers. Additionally, it provides a set of local control interfaces for synchronous viewing and management of servers.
The LS708AU / LS716AU LCD KVM Console feature 17” LCD panels with a resolution of 1280x1024. They also have a durable metal enclosure structure, making them more suitable for specific requirements in various scenarios.
